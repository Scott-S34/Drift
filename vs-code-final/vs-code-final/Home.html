Our Mission

At Drift, we make it effortless and enjoyable to inspire people to achieve sustainability goals and reduce fast fashion consumption.

Fast fashion takes a toll on our environment, polluting our oceans and harming marine life. But together, we can turn the tide. By supporting sustainable practices and mindful consumption, we can reduce our environmental footprint and protect ocean life.

Every choice matters. Let’s embrace change, one step at a time — for our oceans, sea life, and future generations.

Benefits of Drift

Reducing Plastic Pollution

Many conventional clothes are made from synthetic fibers like polyester, nylon, and acrylic, which shed microplastics when washed. These microplastics often end up in waterways and eventually the ocean.

Lowering Carbon Emissions

Lowering Carbon Emissions Sustainable clothing brands often use renewable energy, local production, and low-impact materials. Lower emissions helps protect the ocean from rising temperatures and acidification.

Reducing Water Usage

Conventional cotton farming is water-intensive. Alternatives like organic cotton, linen, and recycled fibers require significantly less water, reducing the strain on freshwater sources and preventing oceanic pollution from agricultural runoff.
