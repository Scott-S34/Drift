Activities

Drift’s Clothing Scanner

If you’re shopping online or in-store, simply scan your clothing of choice, and we’ll provide you with a curated selection of more sustainable and eco-friendly alternatives. Discover options made from organic fabrics, recycled materials, or produced by ethical brands committed to fair labour practices. Our goal is to make responsible fashion choices easier and more accessible. By choosing eco-friendly alternatives, you’re not only reducing your carbon footprint but also supporting companies that care about people and the planet. Shop smarter, feel good about your purchase, and join us in making a positive impact on the future of fashion!

Microplastic Alerts

Stay informed and take action against microplastic pollution! Our innovative scanner helps you identify which clothing fibers shed harmful microplastics during wear and washing. Get personalized insights and learn how to make more sustainable fashion choices.

Fashion Detox Challenges

Take the first step toward a more sustainable wardrobe! Our Fast Fashion Detox Challenges help you break free from wasteful shopping habits, discover eco-friendly fashion alternatives, and embrace mindful consumerism. Each challenge is designed to inspire conscious decisions, reduce clothing waste, and support ethical brands.

Ocean Tracker

Track your impact and make your positive change with our Ocean Tracker! Monitor how your sustainable choices affect our oceans and receive personalized feedback on your environmental footprint. Discover alternatives to fast fashion, explore ethical brands, and get tips on maintaining a cleaner wardrobe.
